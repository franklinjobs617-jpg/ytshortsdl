Thumbnail FAQ & Creator Tips

Can I download the thumbnail of a deleted Short?

No, we rely on the live API feed from YouTube. If the Short is deleted, the asset links will expire.

Why is the thumbnail 16:9 for a vertical short?

YouTube often stores the highest quality thumbnail in a standard 16:9 format. You will need to crop the image to the 9:16 aspect ratio (e.g., 720x1280px) manually.
